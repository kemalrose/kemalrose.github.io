Hello!

Welcome to my homepage. I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, Germany, supervised by Bernd Sturmfels and Simon Telen. I am part of the nonlinear algebra research group. My research lies in applied algebraic geometry with strong connections to discrete geometry, tropical geometry, optimization and statistics.
